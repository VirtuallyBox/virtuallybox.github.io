CyberSwing

CyberSwing is a small game where to goal is the reach the other side of the level while avoiding traps with your grapple hook. Additionally, to add more difficult. The player can only hook onto certain places.

For this game I worked on the core gameplay mechanics and designing a controller that fits with the gameplay.
